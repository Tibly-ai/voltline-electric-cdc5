Our Services

Every job on our schematic — from a single circuit repair to a ground-up commercial build-out — is handled by licensed journeyman electricians with the same precision. Below is the full range of work we take on.

01

Panel Upgrades & Service Changes

200-amp upgrades, meter/service relocations, and load calculations for homes adding EVs, spas, or additions. Built to current code, inspected and permitted.

02

Whole-Home & Partial Rewiring

Aluminum-to-copper conversions, knob-and-tube removal, and remodel rewiring with minimal disruption to finished walls and ceilings.

03

Lighting Design & Installation

Layered lighting plans, recessed and architectural fixtures, landscape lighting, and dimming systems tuned for how a room is actually used.

04

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 charger circuits sized for your panel and future vehicles, from garage installs to multi-unit parking structures.

05

Smart Home & Low-Voltage Wiring

Structured wiring for lighting control, audio, security, and networking — coordinated with your integrator from rough-in through trim.

06

Generator & Backup Power

Standby generator hookups, transfer switches, and battery backup integration so critical circuits stay live through an outage.

07

Commercial Tenant Improvements

Office, retail, and restaurant build-outs — power distribution, dedicated circuits, and lighting scheduled around your opening date.

08

Troubleshooting & Repairs

Flickering lights, tripped breakers, dead outlets, and mystery gremlins — diagnosed and fixed, not just patched over.

09

Inspections & Code Compliance

Pre-sale electrical inspections, insurance re-inspections, and corrective work to bring older systems up to current code.
